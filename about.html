This site was created to log my thoughts on the things that interest me and also to encourage discourse with others.

I have worked on some interesting projects in the past and I'm constantly looking for new and challenging problems to solve.

I'm an enthusiast of open source and free software and try to contribute to projects whenever possible. I'm currently the maintainer of the SCSS Syntax Bundle package for TextMate, Sublime Text and Atom.
